# Payroll and Dues Generation Audit

## Scope checked

- Driver payroll
- Employee payroll
- Hourly driver attendance payroll
- Vehicle owner rent
- Vehicle-assigned-driver payment
- Trip outstanding balances
- Fuel recharge dues
- Duplicate prevention and record deletion

## Problems found and corrected

1. **Weekly, Daily, Contract and Other salary tenures were not calculated correctly.**
   - Drivers previously received the saved amount regardless of non-hourly tenure.
   - Employees previously received the saved amount except for a fixed hourly calculation.
   - The monthly generator now creates one aggregate due per person and selected month while respecting the saved tenure.

2. **A partial trip update could delete dues belonging to other trips.**
   - Trip due cleanup now runs only for an explicit legacy replace-all request.
   - Saving or updating one trip changes only that trip's due.

3. **Deleting a trip or fuel recharge could leave an orphan due.**
   - Explicit deletion now removes the linked due and ownership metadata in the same transaction.

4. **Fuel dues could remain after a recharge returned to Draft or became zero.**
   - The linked fuel due is now removed unless the recharge is Submitted with a positive amount.

5. **Vehicle rental and assigned-driver payment were combined even when their payment cycles differed.**
   - Vehicle owner rent and assigned-driver payment are now generated as separate monthly due records.

6. **Concurrent generation needed a stronger application-level safeguard.**
   - All generated records use deterministic codes.
   - The existing `fleet_dues.code` database UNIQUE constraint is the final duplicate guard.
   - Duplicate-key races are handled safely.

## Monthly calculation rules

Exactly one generated payroll/rental due is stored for each eligible entity and selected month.

- **Monthly:** saved rate × 1 month
- **Weekly:** saved weekly rate × days in selected month ÷ 7
- **Daily:** saved daily rate × number of calendar days in selected month
- **Hourly driver:** not generated by the monthly button; one due is synchronized per completed attendance log
- **Hourly employee:** saved hourly rate × 200 hours, preserving the project's existing basis because there is no employee-attendance module
- **Contract:** one saved contract amount for the selected month
- **Other:** one saved amount for the selected month

## Deterministic duplicate-safe codes

- Driver payroll: `PAY-DRV-{driver}-{YYYY-MM}`
- Employee payroll: `PAY-EMP-{employee}-{YYYY-MM}`
- Vehicle owner rent: `RENT-VHL-{vehicle}-{YYYY-MM}`
- Vehicle driver payment: `RENT-DRV-{vehicle}-{YYYY-MM}`
- Hourly attendance payroll: `PAY-LOG-{log}`
- Trip balance: `DUE-TRP-{trip}`
- Fuel recharge: `DUE-FUEL-{recharge}`

Re-running the same month preserves existing and paid payroll records and does not create another row.
